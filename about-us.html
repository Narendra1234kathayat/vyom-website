About Us

SVIT

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Institute of Technology, Vasad, or SVIT-VASAD, is a private technical institute located on the bank of the Mahi River, Gujarat, India. It offers undergraduate and post-graduate-level technical education. It is managed by The New English School Trust (NEST). The New English School trust after careful considerations decided to establish this self-financed degree engineering college with the support of eminent people of this area.

NEST decided to name the college as the SARDAR VALLABHBHAI PATEL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (SVIT) as a tribute to the great leader Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel who hailed from this area. The Trust formed the Board of Management for the college as per guidelines of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

The Trust requested Prof. Shantibhai Amin, a philanthropic-administrator, to take the responsibility of establishing the college as Chairman of Board of Management. The Board of Management consisted of the technocrats, the educationalists, the industrialists, the social workers, the business magnets of Vasad and Shri. Shivabhai Patel who had a rich experience of managing co-operative organization. SVIT runs various courses that are approved by AICTE, Delhi and affiliated to Gujarat Technological University, Ahmedabad and a some courses are affiliated to Sardar Patel University, Vallbh Vidyanagar.

MCA @ SVIT

The MCA Program commenced at SVIT-Vasad from the year 2003 onwards, with an initial intake of 60 seats, affiliated to Gujarat University, Ahmedabad. From the year 2009 onwards, this program is affiliated to Gujarat Technological University, Ahmedabad and approved by AICTE-New Delhi. Currently, the MCA program is a 2 Year full-time PG program with an intake of 60 students effective from the academic year 2020-21. The admission eligibility, procedure, etc, is governed by ACPC, Ahmedabad. Apart from having good infrastructural facilities like State of the Art Laboratories, a Well Equipped Library, etc., the program is further reinforced with a team of highly qualified, dedicated and motivated faculty members and supporting staff. A lot of efforts and activities are undertaken to provide industrial interaction to students and to provide good IDP (final year project) and Job Placement opportunities to the students.Apart from Curricular activities, students are also given exposure to sports, inter-collegiate events/competitions, etc. for their holistic development.
